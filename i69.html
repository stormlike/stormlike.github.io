Ion Keyboards i69

Dark grey 65% with XT columns case from Ion/Illuminati with a solid brass weight. Custom plate in carbon fiber and PCB from Max from Cable Car Designs. The keyboard was not meant for ISO so thats why I had to arrange a commision for custom PCB and plate. The keyboard was a small private GB so there's only ten i69:s out there, and this is the only one with ISO. Gateron Silent Ink switches make the board very silent and the dark grey anodization is a very good color since almost all caps looks good on it.

When the I took these pictures I used GMK TA 90 with the super nice R5 profile on bottom row, but have since then moved on to GMK Space Cadet to get the uniform colored F-keys
