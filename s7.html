S7 Elephant

I missed the R1 of the S7 Elephant when it ran and thought it looked just gorgeous, so when R2 appeared as IC/GB late 2019 I jumped on it straight away.
I bought the blockered version in E-white finish together with two PCBs and one white aluminium plate and one brass plate.
Put in Mill-Max sockets in one of the PCBs and built the keyboard with that and the brass plate.
It is for sure not an EDC (every day carry) keyboard since it weighs many kilos, but perfect for the home office.

Features:

  • Layout: 65% keyboard with blocker at arrows and an additional XT column to the left
  • Finish: E-white
  • Plate: Brass
  • Mount: Top mount
  • PCB: S7 Elephant PCB with Mini-USB
  • Stabilizers: Smokey Durocks with gold wire, lubed with 205g0
  • Switches: NK Creams lubed with 205g0
  • Keycaps: 7bit R6 SA VoC
  • Weight: Brass
