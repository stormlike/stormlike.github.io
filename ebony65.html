Ebony65

Custom ebony 65% keyboard

This is a custom build using a Mexican ebony case by a Redditor from the US, carbon fiber plate from a guy in Netherlands and a PCB from yet another guy in Germany. All the parts has not showed up yet though so currently is it a Tada68 PCB and plate in the build. The final build will also not have the six 1u keycaps to the right of the spacebar but 1.5-1.5-1-1-1 instead.

The switches are milky Gateron blacks, lubed with Tribosys 3203, and the stabilizers are GMK ones. Keycaps in the picture above is GMK Yuri with orange ISO enter and Esc.

The case is of course very heavy due to the high density of the Mexican ebony. It also very scratch and dent-resistant.
