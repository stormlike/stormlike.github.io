Melody96

Got in on a group buy by Ymdk for a case and plate setup. Chose blue upper part of case and gold lower part and plate. Took a few month but it finally arrived. The case is called Melody96 and was sold with a PCB as well, but that PCB was fitted with USB-C and I want Mini-USB. Got them to remove the PCB from the order. I still had a Ymd96 PCB since before when I ordered two of them. The keyboard is topmounted; meaning the plate screws into the top of the case and lastly the bottom is screwed in place.

It’s a heavy case with the brass weight (removable) in the bottom. The keyboard is fitted with lubed (Krytox GPL104) milky Gateron yellows and GMK stabilizers.
