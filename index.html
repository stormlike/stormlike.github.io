About this site

This is just a way to catalogue my ever growing collection of mechanical keyboards with some noteworthy information regarding each. Order is by completion date. Newest first.

Keyboards

Use dropdown menu above for quick selection, or scroll down through the page to find the different keyboards currently built. Current builds or unbuilt keyboards not noted.

Short info

All my keyboards are built with ISO layout except for the ortho boards. Most of them have stepped capslock and the smaller ones usually have split right shift.
