Information

This page lists my ever-changing collection of cameras and lenses I have and use. The page is built with some filtering possibilities for mount, zoom/prime, and such. When it comes to brands and systems, the Fuji GFX digital medium format is for high resolution prepared shots while the MFT(Micro 4/3) system is for wildlife, birds, video and when portability and speed is wanted.
Check out my Instagram and Flickr photostream for photos.
