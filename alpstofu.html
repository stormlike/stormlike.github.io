Alps Tofu

This is an Alps build with unlubed green alps, steel plate that was cut from a 100% keyboard and Dell Alps caps. The black case is a Kbdfans traymount Tofu. Not much to say about it.

It doesn's sound or feel horrible, but it's not one I would like to use daily.

Update June 2020
