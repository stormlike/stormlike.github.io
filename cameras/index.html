Cameras and lenses

Info

A list of the cameras and lenses that's currently in use by me.

Cameras:

  • Canon EOS RP: 26.2 MP Full frame primary camera
  • Canon EOS M6 Mark II: 32.5 MP APS-C for 4K video as well as backup camera
  • Canon EOS M: 18 MP APS-C small EDC pocket camera

Lenses:

  • Canon EF 85mm f/1.2L II USM: Portrait and low light (all)
  • Canon EF 24-105mm f/4L IS II USM: Standard zoom (all)
  • Canon RF 35mm f/1.8 Macro IS STM: Cityscapes and allround (RP only)
  • Samyang MF 14mm f/2.8 RF: Ultra wide angle manual lens for landscapes (RP only)
  • Tamron 70-210mm f/4 Di VC USD: Telephoto zoom (All)
  • Sigma 105mm f/2.8 EX DG OS HSM Macro: Macro lens (All)
  • Canon EF-M 18-150mm f/3.5-6.3 IS STM: Super zoom (M6 MkII and M)
  • 7Artisans 35mm f/1.2: Fast, manual focus small lens (M6 MkII and M)
