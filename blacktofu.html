Black Tofu

This is a very simple traymount Tofu build with aluminium plate, but the color combinations really does it for me. The black case, the dark modifiers with their colorful legends and the bright alphas make it subtle yet striking. The lubed Revo Clear switches combined with a dampening mat in the case make it sound thoccy and good. GMK Screw-in stabilizers of course.

The keycaps are a combination of ultra sonic cleaned original Cherry keycaps harvested from an old keyboard and new GMK Solarized Dark modifiers with the colorful legends.
