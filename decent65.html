Upcoming: Decent65

Decent65 v2 prototype

This is the result of the prototyping of Decent65 where me and the engineer soran tweaked some of the features. Or, well, I suggested some changes and he realized them, and then added some even better ones. This keyboard is being made by Salvun and will hopefully within a reasonable timespan go live via IC (interest check) on Geekhack to collect thoughts and potential interest.

The overall design is the same and it will still be top-mounted and have the same low front achieved using a ledge. Some of the changes are practical, others are aesthetical.

The changes from v1 are most notably:

  • Slightly change side curve
  • Daughterboard for connector and therefor also USB-C
  • Blocker between modifiers and arrows
  • Increase from 6 to 7 degree angle
  • Alignment-pins for top and bottom
Current state: Prototype is being machined and anodized. PCB is being designed. More info to come.
