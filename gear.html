Information

This page lists my ever-changing collection of cameras and lenses I have and use. The page is built with some filtering possibilities for mount, zoom/prime, and such. When it comes to brands and systems, the Fuji GFX digital medium format is for high resolution prepared shots while the Sony system is for wildlife, birds and video. The MFT systems are mostly for webcam/streaming.
Check out my Instagram and Flickr photostream for photos. Cameras and lenses I traded in or sold are found here.
