Acrylic Tofu

This is a very simple traymount Tofu build with brass plate, but the combination of frosted acrylic case and the keycaps make it quite nice. Switches are lubed vintage Cherry blacks and the stabs are GMK screw-ins. The plate is actually black aluminium in the pictures, but was changed shortly after pictures were taken.

The keycaps are GMK Nautilus R1 with a little hapster cap on escape.
