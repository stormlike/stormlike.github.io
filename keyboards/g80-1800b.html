Black G(H)80-1800

I got hold of yet another old unused Cherry G80-1800 keyboard, but this time it was a black Lauer Fischer branded one. I got it from Maarten who also supplied a GH80-1800 replacement PCB making this USB-enabled and programmable using QMK. Put in the vintage Cherry MX black switches from the previous G80-1800 keyboard

The keycaps are GMK White on black alphas and most of the mods are blue GMK Space Cadets where the rest are WoB.
